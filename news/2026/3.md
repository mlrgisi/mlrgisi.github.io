# Congratulations Dr. Kushal Bose!

Delighted to announce that [Kushal Bose](https://kushalbose92.github.io/) successfully defended his PhD thesis at ISI Kolkata!

**Thesis:** "[Innovations in Graph Neural Network Design: Addressing Oversmoothing, Heterophily, and Information Propagation](https://digitalcommons.isical.ac.in/doctoral-theses/636/)"

Kushal delivered an impressive defense, answering questions with clarity and confidence before his supervisor Prof. Swagatam Das, external examiner Prof. Jaya Sil (IIEST, Shibpur), Prof. Utpal Garain (ISI, Kolkata) and attendees.

**Research Highlights:**
- 1 TETCI paper
- 1 TNNLS paper
- 1 TAI paper

Kushal will shortly join **ELLIS Institute Finland** as **Postdoctoral Fellow** under the principal investigation of [Dr. Michael Cochez](https://www.cochez.nl/), where he continues to apply his expertise in graph machine learning. Wishing him continued success and impact in his career!
