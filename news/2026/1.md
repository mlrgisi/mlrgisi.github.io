# Congratulations Dr. Anish Chakrabarty!

Delighted to announce that [Anish Chakrabarty](https://chakrabartyanish.faculty.bio/) successfully defended his PhD thesis on February 4, 2026, at ISI Kolkata! [Awarded on Feb 17, 2026](https://youtu.be/c3RkQwfWSJk?t=3857).

**Thesis:** "[Statistical Guarantees of Deep Generative Models Involving Diverse Spaces: Generation Consistency and Robustness](https://dspace.isical.ac.in/jspui/handle/10263/7646)"

Anish, an SRF in the Statistics and Mathematics Unit, delivered an outstanding defense at the ECSU Seminar Room, answering questions with clarity and confidence before his supervisors (Prof. Probal Chaudhuri and Prof. Swagatam Das), external examiner Prof. Pabitra Mitra (IIT Kharagpur), RFAC members, and attendees.

**Research Highlights:**
- 2 NeurIPS papers (including 1 Spotlight)
- 1 ICML paper
- 1 AISTATS paper
- Journal publications in *Statistics and Computing*, *Stat*, and *Statistics and Probability Letters*

Anish will soon begin a postdoctoral position in a joint project between **École Polytechnique and Paris Telecom**. Wishing him a brilliant and impactful future ahead!
