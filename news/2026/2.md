# Congratulations Dr. Faizanuddin Anasari!

Delighted to announce that [Faizanuddin Anasari](https://www.isical.ac.in/~faizanuddin_r/) successfully defended his PhD thesis at ISI Kolkata!

**Thesis:** "[Generalization under Sub-population Shift: Equitable Models for Imbalanced, Long-tailed, and Fair Representation Learning](https://dspace.isical.ac.in/jspui/handle/10263/7648)"

Faizanuddin delivered an impressive defense, answering questions with clarity and confidence before his supervisor Prof. Swagatam Das, external examiner Prof. Sanjoy Kumar Saha (Jadavpur University), and attendees.

**Research Highlights:**
- 1 MICCAI papers
- 1 ISBI papers
- 2 IJCNN papers
- 1 EAAI papers
- 2 IJCAI papers

Faizanuddin is currently employed as a **Data & AI Scientist** at **Philips Healthcare**, where he continues to apply his expertise in equitable and fair machine learning. Wishing him continued success and impact in his career!
